# Finzly brand voice & identity

Finzly, Inc. builds **BankOS** — an AI + infrastructure "innovation core" that lets banks and
credit unions modernize **without ripping out their core**. Everything written here must make
content sound like Finzly: confident, forward-looking, data-grounded, and useful to banking
decision-makers. Website: <https://finzly.com/>

## Who we talk to (audience)

B2B, never consumers. Primary readers are decision-makers at:
- **Banks and credit unions** (community/regional through large), plus their **corporate clients**.
- **Fintechs** building on banking rails (BaaS / embedded banking).

Speak peer-to-peer to people who run payments, treasury, digital, and innovation. Assume
fluency — they know what RTP and ISO 20022 are. Sell outcomes (speed, new revenue, lower cost,
less risk), not jargon for its own sake.

## Voice & tone

Finzly sounds like a **confident, credible insider who is optimistic about the future of banking**.

- **Punchy, declarative openers.** Short hook line that states a truth or tension.
  _"Innovation moves fast. Progress should move all of us forward."_
- **Data-grounded.** Cite real proof — analyst reports, polls, named sources.
  _"McKinsey's recent global banking report highlights that ~70% of banking innovation is
  concentrated in crowded, high-popularity areas."_
- **Forward-looking POV.** Frame Finzly as helping banks "lead what's next" — AI, tokenized money,
  real-time, unified ledger.
- **Helpful, not hypey.** Educate first; the product is the answer to a real problem, not a
  miracle. Avoid breathless superlatives (see compliance: no "fastest/guaranteed/100%").
- **A little urgency, earned.** _"First-mover advantages don't stay open forever."_ Use sparingly.
- **Clear CTA.** End posts with a next step: read the blog, message us, book time, learn more.

Rhythm: hook → insight (often with a stat) → Finzly's POV/product as the enabler → CTA + link.

## Vocabulary — say it exactly

**Company / core**
- **Finzly** — always capitalized, never "Finsley/Finzley". The logo wordmark is lowercase
  `finzly` but in prose write **Finzly**.
- **BankOS** (one word, capital B/OS) — "the innovation core for banks and credit unions."
  Tagline: _"Modern banks run on Finzly BankOS."_

**The Galaxy suite** (composable modules on BankOS):
- **Account Galaxy** — accounts/deposits
- **Payment Galaxy** — multi-rail payments (FedNow, RTP, ACH, wires)
- **Token Galaxy** — unified fiat + **tokenized money** movement (stablecoins, digital assets)
- **Digital Galaxy** — digital banking experiences
- **Trade Galaxy** — trade / FX

**Themes & terms we own:** unified money movement, **unified ledger** (for both fiat and digital
assets), real-time / 24/7 payments, tokenized money, stablecoins, multi-rail, payment
modernization, embedded banking / BaaS, Pay-by-Bank, **no core replacement**, composable.

**Signature taglines** (reuse, don't mangle):
- "Modern banks run on Finzly BankOS."
- "Your bank. Your way. Composable by design."
- "Build your bank your way."
- "Unified money movement is here."

## Visual identity (for branded images)

- **Colors:** electric **violet-purple** (~`#5B21E0`) + **black**, on **clean white** (or deep
  purple gradient for dark scenes). Purple is the accent; keep it dominant but not noisy.
- **Logo:** lowercase `finzly` wordmark with the purple "wing" mark.
  - Use `nano-banana-image-gen/assets/finzly-logo-dark-black-purple.png` on **light** backgrounds.
  - Use `nano-banana-image-gen/assets/finzly-logo-light-white-purle.png` on **dark** backgrounds.
  - Default placement: **top-left or top-right**, clear margin, colors/proportions unchanged.
- **Style:** modern, clean, minimal fintech. Soft geometric shapes, glowing nodes/rings, subtle
  grids, line icons (bank, shield, payment, ledger). Avoid clutter, stock-photo cheese, and busy
  gradients. Iconography is flat/line-based and purple-accented.
- Pair the brand voice with `nano-banana-image-gen` to generate matching hero/poster images.

Finzly posts use a focused cluster of topical hashtags (not a wall). Draw from:
`#Finzly #BankingInnovation #BankOS #unifiedledger #tokenizedmoney #stablecoins #paymentsplatform
#unifiedfiat #DigitalBanking #FedNow #RTP #embeddedbanking`. Pick 3–6 relevant to the post; add
event/timely tags as needed (e.g. `#DigitalBanking` for a conference).

## Dos & don'ts

**Do**
- Lead with a strong hook; keep sentences tight.
- Back claims with named data/sources; link to blogs/resources.
- Center the bank's outcome; position Finzly/BankOS as the enabler.
- Use the exact product names and approved taglines.
- End with a clear CTA.

**Don't**
- Don't imply Finzly is a bank or holds deposits — Finzly is the **technology/infrastructure**.
- Don't use unsubstantiated superlatives or guarantees (see [[finzly-compliance]]).
- Don't write consumer-y, salesy, or hype-filled copy ("revolutionary!!!", emoji spam).
- Don't misspell the brand or invent product names.
- Don't talk down to the audience or over-explain basics.
