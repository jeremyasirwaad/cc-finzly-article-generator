# Finzly Marketing Content Studio

This project generates publish-ready marketing content (LinkedIn posts, blog articles, Instagram captions, press-release drafts) and matching poster/hero images for Finzly, Inc. Every piece of content produced in this repo must sound like Finzly and respect the brand and compliance rules.

## Foundation rules — load these for any content task

Two skills are the source of truth. Read/apply them before drafting or reviewing ANY public-facing content or branded image:

- **`finzly-brand-voice`** — voice, vocabulary, product names (BankOS, the Galaxy suite), taglines, audience, visual identity (black + violet-purple, logo usage), dos & don'ts.
- **`finzly-compliance`** — guardrails every external piece must pass before it's "publish-ready": claim substantiation, banned superlatives/guarantees, the banking-license boundary, accurate security/compliance language, and a pre-publish checklist.

Images are produced with the **`nano-banana-image-gen`** skill; all image outputs go to `image-output/`. Reusable image prompts live in `image-prompts/`.
