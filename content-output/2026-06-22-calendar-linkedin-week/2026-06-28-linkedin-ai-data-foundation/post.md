## Caption (publish-ready)

Every bank has an AI budget for 2026. Most are spending it one layer too high.

The pitch is everywhere: agentic AI moving from pilots to production, autonomous workflows running operations end to end. The promise is real. The precondition is boring.

AI is only as good as the data underneath it — and most banks' data isn't ready. ABA Banking Journal reports that 21% of bank data is duplicated and 28% of legacy applications remain undocumented (April 2026). Point an AI agent at a fragmented payments stack and you automate the confusion.

This is the part nobody demos: the unified data and money-movement layer that AI actually needs to be useful in a bank.

That's the role BankOS is built to play — the innovation core and unified ledger that bring payments, accounts, and money movement together, so the AI you deploy on top has clean, real-time data to work with. Finzly doesn't sell AI as a feature. It builds the foundation that makes AI worth deploying.

The banks that win with AI in 2026 will be the ones that fixed the plumbing first.
